== Description ==

The <strong>InkGo Product Personalizer for WooCommerce</strong> is a plugin for WooCommerce, this plugin allows you sell personalized print-on-demand products on WooComemrce. Your customer can change text, photo and live preview on mockup 3D before buying.

<strong>Ready-for-print:</strong> InkGo automatic create file ready-for-print and saved in order, you only download file PNG with 300 DPI and print.

<strong>Print-on-Demand and Dropshipping integrations:</strong> import products from your favorite print-on-demand catalogs and have them personalized by your customers using InkGo. Products are fulfilled and shipped by your favorite services. Integrates with Printful, CustomCat, DreamShip, WC Fulfillment, GearLaunch...and any your fulfillment services.

== Installation ==
1. Unzip the downloaded zip file.
2. Upload the plugin folder into the 'wp-content/plugins/' directory of your WordPress site.
3. Activate 'InkGo Product Personalizer for WooCommerce' from Plugins page
4. Go to menu "InkGo"
5. Click button "Connect"
6. Create account on InkGo and complete install.

== Main Functions ==
Start a free today and see how these customizations look in your store:

**Products type**
InkGo Product Personalizer for WooCommerce lets customers personalize any product (clothing, promotional gifts, paper products, shoes, jewelry, watches) and visualize how they’ll look before checking out.

**Customizations**
- Text customization: You can add text to your products, change its color, size, orientation, rotation and choose their favorite fonts. Your customer only need change and buy.

- Own images: customers can upload their own images and use them to create uniquely personal products.

- Product variations: allow visitors to change colors, sizes and styles to match exactly what they want.

- Live Preview: automatic update design of customer on mockup 3D. When your customers can see a live preview of their personalization they feel better about the buying experience and trust you and your store more.

- Print-ready files: InkGo automatic create file output of customize and saved to your order. You can go to page view order and download file output of 300 DPI (print-ready)

Print-on-Demand and Dropshipping integrations: import products from your favorite print-on-demand catalogs and have them personalized by your customers using InkGo. Products are fulfilled and shipped by your favorite services. Integrates with Printful, CustomCat, DreamShip, WC Fulfillment, GearLaunch...and any your fulfillment services.

- Fully responsive that optimized on tablet and mobile browser.

- Swatch: beautiful Color, Image and Buttons variation swatches for woocommerce product attributes
